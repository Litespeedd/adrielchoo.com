AI · Founder · End-to-End Product Ownership

Black Lotus UX —
UX Intelligence Agency

Built in parallel with a Fortune 500 role, Black Lotus UX is a boutique AI-powered UX intelligence agency — and a live case study in what happens when a designer decides to build the tools the industry is missing.

Six specialized AI agents handle the complete delivery stack: WCAG compliance auditing across entire site crawls, competitive positioning scored across six dimensions, heuristic analysis, Playwright PDF report generation, and role-adaptive client outreach — all triggered from a single Slack command. Client reports surface on a live dashboard with score-drop alerts and PDF exports.

The business itself is designed like a product: four service tiers with clear pricing, Profit First banking infrastructure, and a complete set of NYC-reviewed legal documents. What used to take a UX consultant weeks to deliver manually now runs overnight.
